Who we are

ACOSEIN FOUNDATION CLG (Awakening Widows, People Living with Disabilities (PWDs), and Communities on Socio-Economic Initiatives) supports vulnerable and underserved groups through empowerment, health promotion, inclusive education, and sustainable livelihoods—anchored in community participation and dignity.

Vision • Mission

Vision: Cultivating stronger communities through inclusive education, engagement, opportunity creation, and addressing local needs.

Mission: To act as a catalyst for transformative change by empowering individuals and communities to achieve sustainable development and prosperity.

Core Focus Areas

Programs that drive resilience, dignity, and opportunity.

Empowerment • Healthcare • Conservation • Education
  • Economic & Socio-Cultural Empowerment Community savings & loaning (VSLA), skills training, entrepreneurship mentorship, and practical capacity building for sustainable household income.
  • Healthcare Initiatives Household health & hygiene promotion, immunization awareness, health outreach and referrals, and improved access to quality care for rural women and underserved communities.
  • Environmental Conservation Tree planting, awareness campaigns, community stewardship, and protection of wetlands and water catchment areas.
  • Education Programs Early childhood development, support for learning resources, and strengthening parent/guardian engagement. (Core focus: “Learn, Play and Thrive” pilot program.)
  • Community & Livelihood Development Dignified shelter support, nutrition and food security, and livelihood value chains including poultry and bee products.
  • GBV Advocacy • Child Protection & Safeguarding Public awareness, survivor support and referrals, safeguarding education, and community-based protection frameworks.

Flagship Projects

Medical and community initiatives designed for long-term impact.

Specialist Doctors Plaza • Level 5 Specialist Hospital

Specialist Doctors Plaza

A medical project aimed at expanding access to specialist services, diagnostics and coordinated care. We seek partnerships for planning, equipment, staffing, and sustainability.

Level 5 Specialist Hospital

A proposed advanced care facility to improve regional healthcare outcomes through specialist clinics, modern infrastructure, and patient-centered services.

Eco-Health & Community Resilience

Integrating health outreach, environmental stewardship, and social protection to strengthen households and communities. (See pitch deck and concept notes in our Documents section.)
